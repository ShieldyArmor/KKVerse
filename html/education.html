About the education page

In this page I will talk about my past experiences and things worth mentioning about my past education. Ranging from personal development to something more closely related to things that might affect my future work life.

Hagaløkka Skole (Pre-school)

Hagaløkka was my first school I went to, it was meant for kids from all countries and cultures to have a place to learn Norwegian together, I have never went to school or kindergarten so it was an interesting experience to see all of these kids from different countries. The most memorable experience from here is the start of my creative and artistic interest, as well as first proper friendships.

Nesøya Skole

Coming to Nesøya in the middle of the first half-year of 1st grade was interesting, I was never used to being the center of attention of strangers and it was a feeling I will never forget. For the most part my interest in video games peaked in this era and the same goes with my artistic side, where I would often be asked to stop drawing during lessons because of how much I was into it.

Landøya ungdomsskole

What was the most interesting about Landøya is the change of mind from a lot of people. There were many issues when it came to interaction with other kids towards the end of Nesøya, including bullying for the last few years due to my nationality and cultural differences. Even though I still wish I had my current mindset back then and hopefully try to reason. Regarding work and other experiences, This is where my Traditional/Digital art interest dropped in favor of Programming.

Bleiker videregående skole

Year 1 of Bleiker is currently where I am at, studying IT and Media production and working hard for a good grade in advanced math which I will need in the future when going to university. I have recently picked up digital art yet again and found a new love in making short films and editing videos on the side. Despite the amount of bad things I have heard about Bleiker before going here. I believe that this was the best choice I could have made when it comes to picking a school for me.
